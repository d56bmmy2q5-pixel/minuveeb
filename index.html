About

Anna Elisabeth Remmelg is a Tallinn-based graphic designer. She enjoys playful approaches and working with typography. She particularly likes working with books and printed matter, while also exploring moving image, posters, and visual identities. She studies Graphic Design at the Estonian Academy of Arts and has also spent one semester studying Graphic Design at the Royal Academy of Fine Arts in Antwerp.


Feel free to contact me via email or instagram.
